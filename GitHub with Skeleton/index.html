More Work From Me

Check out additional design work by me below! These are a few of my favorite works, ranging from packaging/branding design to UI design.


Vitamins Packaging

A fresh and fun redesign for a vitamin brand called Align. With this project, I envisioned my product standing out on the shelf of the vitamin aisle because of its eye-catching color, simplicity, and fun personality. Catering towards younger wellness-center individuals who enjoy taking care of their health and enjoy following health trends. My design utilizes bright colors, bold type, and an engaging tone of voice.



Zucko Brand Identity

For this project, I was given a list of made-up brands to chose from and design a brand identity for. This brand identity includes a logo, website, social posts, packaging, and additional mockups. The made-up brand I chose was Zucko, a zucchini cooking oil brand. My vision for Zucko was a company that values clean and honest ingredients, targeting its audience toward health-conscious individuals. I also envisioned Zucko as a small business that grows their zucchinis on their farmstead and delivers their cooking oil to grocery stores. Zucko is a lively, enthusiastic brand that you can rely on as your go-to healthy cooking oil alternative.



Goodreads UI Redesign

During this UI project, I was tasked to give a “makeover” to an outdated mobile application of my choice using Figma. After undergoing light research and testing, I designed a fresh and new visual aesthetic for the app, Goodreads. This makeover includes reworking the user flow and a reapplication of the branding including typography, buttons, colors, grid/layout, icons, UI components, and photos. My goal was to redirect Goodreads target audience towards younger readers who want to explore and share their books with their friends.
